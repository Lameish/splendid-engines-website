People follow people.

Buyers don't make decisions based on what brands say. They follow the people they trust. When you're not part of those conversations, you're not part of the deal.

Our story

In B2B, as in life, people follow people. Roughly 90% of B2B purchasing decisions are peer-influenced. Splendid was founded on a simple bet: if you put your customers' trusted experts at the center of your marketing, the deals follow.

We built Splendid because the old playbook of cold outreach, gated content, and SDR armies is broken. Modern revenue leaders need a system that treats buyers like adults: meet them where they're already learning, with people they already trust.

How we work

Five things we believe

We say no often

We only take on clients we're confident we can move the needle for. If we're not the right fit, we'll tell you and point you somewhere better.

We respect your time

Every meeting has a purpose. Every deliverable earns its keep. Automation handles the rote work so humans can focus on the relationships.

We write for humans

Every word is meant for a real person to read. No AI slop. No SEO listicles. No content factory output.

We measure what matters

Pipeline and revenue, not vanity metrics. Decisions are anchored in data, not opinions and feelings.

We play long games

Trust compounds. Reputation compounds. Networks compound. We're building for the next decade, not the next quarter.
