Our thesis

In B2B, buyers trust their peers far more than they'll ever trust your brand. The best demand programs put peer voices at the center, not the edge. We build the engine that makes that work at scale.

The engine

A three-step system

Step 01

Map your target community and the voices that shape it.

We build a live model of your TAM, then surface the ex-practitioners, analysts, and operators your buyers already follow. Influence isn't generic. It's specific to your category, and we find the specific people.

Step 02

Pitch, land, and co-create category-defining content.

We approach each voice with a real reason to participate. Our team runs the production end-to-end: interview, draft, design, ship. Your brand earns the credit by being the publisher; the content earns attention because the bylines do.

Step 03

Measure qualified interactions at target accounts — and feed the cycle.

We report on the metric your board cares about: who from your target accounts engaged, at what depth, and on which piece. Every interactor becomes a candidate co-creator for the next round. The engine fuels itself.
